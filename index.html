About

Scholarship with heart

Dr. Maya Ellis examines reproductive science and technology through a feminist lens, studying how design decisions, data models, and protocols amplify or correct systemic bias. Her research uncovers the social lives of lab work, emphasizing accountability to the patients and communities most affected by reproductive innovation.

She is a Senior Fellow at the Center for Feminist Futures, where she co-develops collaborative research roadmaps with clinicians, community doulas, and technologists to ensure reproductive technologies are guided by justice and care.
